Brand Anti-Patterns
What Peloton Is Not
01
No Before & After Photos
We don't reduce members to their bodies. Transformation is emotional, not physical.
02
No Cheesy Ads
No stock-photo fitness. Our creative earns the same attention as our classes.
03
Not a Fad
No trend-chasing. Peloton is a durable platform, not this year's fitness craze.
04
Don't Talk Down
Never preachy, never superior. We celebrate — we do not shame or lecture.

Brand Pillars 04 & 05
Wholly Immersive & Committed
04
Wholly Immersive
Marketing always shows the protagonist fully engaged and immersed in class — not distracted, not multitasking. This is their escape. Content is the hero.
The class is a world they enter — not an activity they do.
05
Committed
Creative shows the work and the intensity. Peloton users are wholeheartedly dedicated — not casual exercisers, not dabblers. Sweat is required proof.
Show the effort. Show what it costs. Show what it produces.

Precision Audience Targeting

Reach the right students at the right schools—at the dormitory level.

thefacebook.com allows advertisers to target users through traditional banner placements and contextual links, with audience segmentation unavailable on any other platform. Because every user self-reports their school, concentration, courses, dormitory, interests, and political views, campaigns can be filtered to an extraordinarily precise slice of the college population. Ads can be scoped to a single university, a class year, a major, or a zip code—or any combination of all of the above.

Targeting built on declared identity, not inferred behavior.
thefacebook.com ad targeting draws on data no other platform has access to: students' declared academic identity — school, courses, concentration, class year — combined with explicit social graph connections and verified institutional affiliations. Campaigns can reach users at their dorm, work, home, or library, and can be targeted on any combination of 17 parameters. We work directly with advertisers to identify the most effective placements for each campaign goal.

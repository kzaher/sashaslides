Business Model

We take a 10% commission on each completed transaction. No subscription fees. No listing fees. Revenue is earned only when a booking is made.

10%
Commission per transaction
Charged to the traveler at checkout. Zero friction for hosts — they list once and receive payment minus our fee.
$25
Average fee per booking

Adoption Strategy

Three channels that reach budget-conscious travelers where they are already concentrated.

Events
Target high-density events with a listing widget
Octoberfest (6M attendees), CeBIT (700K), Summerfest (1M), Euro Cup (3M+), Mardi Gras (800K). Hotel capacity collapses at these events — we deploy the widget 8 weeks prior to capture demand at its peak.
Partnerships
Cheap and alternative travel platforms
GoLoCo, KAYAK, Orbitz — distribution partnerships that embed our inventory inside platforms travelers are already using to find budget options.
Craigslist
Dual-posting feature leverages 50K weekly listings
Hosts who post on Craigslist can cross-list on AirBed&Breakfast in one click — converting existing supply with zero acquisition cost.

# AirBed&Breakfast

Book rooms with locals, rather than hotels.

## Problem

- Price is an important concern for customers booking travel online.
- Hotels leave you disconnected from the city and its culture.
- No easy way exists to book a room with a local or become a host.

## Solution

A web platform where users can rent out their space to host travelers to:
- Save money when traveling
- Make money when hosting
- Share culture -- local connection to the city

## Market Validation

There is already proven demand for this kind of service:
- Couchsurfing.com has 660,000 total users
- Craigslist has 50,000 temporary housing listings per week in the US

## Market Size

- Total Available Market: 2 billion+ trips booked worldwide
- Serviceable Available Market: 560 million budget and online trips
- Share of Market: 84 million trips with AirBed&Breakfast (15% of available market)

## Product

The user flow is simple: search by city, review listings, book it. Stay with a local when traveling.

## Business Model

We take a 10% commission on each transaction.
- 84 million trips (share of market, 15% of available)
- $25 average fee ($80/night average over 3 nights)
- $2.1 billion projected revenue by 2011

## Adoption Strategy

Three go-to-market channels:
- **Events** -- target large events monthly: Octoberfest (6M attendees), Cebit (700K), Summerfest (1M), Eurocup (3M+), Mardi Gras (800K), with a listing widget
- **Partnerships** -- cheap/alternative travel partners: GoLoCo, KAYAK, Orbitz
- **Craigslist** -- dual posting feature to leverage existing demand

## Competition

Competitive landscape on two axes: affordable vs. expensive, and offline vs. online transaction.

Competitors include CouchSurfing, Craigslist, BedandBreakfast.com, Hostels.com, rentahome, Orbitz, hotels.com, rentobi.com, and VRBO.

AirBed&Breakfast is positioned uniquely as affordable + online transaction.

## Competitive Advantages

- **1st to market** for transaction-based temporary housing
- **Host incentive** -- hosts can make money (unlike Couchsurfing)
- **List once** -- hosts post one time with us vs. daily on Craigslist
- **Ease of use** -- search by price, location, and check-in/check-out dates
- **Profiles** -- browse host profiles and book in 3 clicks
- **Design and brand** -- memorable name, launching at historic DNC for share of mind

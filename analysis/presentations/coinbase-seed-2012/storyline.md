# Coinbase: Your Hosted Bitcoin Wallet

## What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a new digital currency. Its key properties:
- Instant
- International
- No transaction fees

Bitcoin is already a global phenomenon with adoption concentrated in the US, Europe, and East Asia.

## Bitcoin is Growing Fast

Bitcoin processes $2 million USD per day in transaction volume, with a strong upward trend from March through August 2012.

## Early Adopter Use Cases

Bitcoin already has real users across many categories: international payments, virtual goods, games, developing world, peer-to-peer, remittance, micro-transactions, and e-commerce.

## The Problem: Too Difficult to Use

Current Bitcoin tools are clunky desktop clients and command-line terminals. Bitcoin is powerful but unusable for normal people.

## The Coinbase Solution

Coinbase is a hosted Bitcoin wallet -- a clean, consumer-friendly web and mobile interface for sending, receiving, and managing Bitcoin. Transaction history, balances, send/receive -- all accessible from any device.

## Positioning

Coinbase is to Bitcoin what iTunes was to MP3. The consumer-friendly layer that makes a powerful but raw technology accessible to everyone.

## Traction: User Growth

Coinbase signups show hockey-stick growth at 20% daily, accelerating from near-zero to thousands of daily signups within two weeks.

## Traction: Transaction Volume

$65,000 USD in transaction volume in the first 5 weeks, with exponential growth in BTC volume peaking near 1,000 BTC. Not just signups -- real money is moving through the platform.
